## Learning tips

- Creating or finding a good summary is very important - because you might need to re-take the test or will need to use the material in a future course.

- Learn from the summaries that are provided in the course site - definitions, theorms, etc. My summaries are inteneded to complete them and provider a complete view of the material.

- Solve tests early - When learning for the test, after you go over the material, start solving tests of the last few years, even if you don't know the material perfectly. Looking at the tests history, their solutions and the material together is very practical and helps you understand tha material quickly.
